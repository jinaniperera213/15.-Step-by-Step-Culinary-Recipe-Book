Main Dishes
Garlic herb Grilled chicken Creamy Chicken Pasta BBQ Chicken Pizza Vegetable Noodles

Ingredients
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges (optional)
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Chicken
    1. Wash and pat dry the chicken breasts
    2. Season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Make the Herb Marinade
    1. Mix garlic, olive oil, oregano, thyme, and paprika in a bowl.
    2. Coat the chicken evenly with the mixture.
    3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill the Chicken
    1. Preheat the grill or grill pan.
    2. Cook the chicken for 6–8 minutes on each side.
    3. Ensure the chicken is fully cooked
  4. Serve
    1. Remove from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes.
    2. Garnish with lemon wedges
    3. Serve hot with vegetables, salad, or rice.
Cooking Time: 45 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy
Servings: 2 people 🍽️

Ingredients
  • 200g pasta
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Preparation Steps
  1. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Add the pasta and cook until al dente
    3. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Chicken
    1. Season the chicken with salt and black pepper.
    2. Heat olive oil in a pan
    3. Cook the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked.
  3. Make the Creamy Sauce
    1. Add minced garlic to the pan and sauté for 1 minute.
    2. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well.
    3. Add Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.
    2. Mix well until the pasta is evenly coated.
    3. Garnish with fresh parsley.
    4. Serve hot and enjoy!
Preparation Time:30 minutes
Difficulty Level:Easy
Servings:4 people 🍽️

Ingredients
  • 1 pizza base
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Toppings
    1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
    2. Mix the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce.
    3. Slice the onion and prepare the other toppings.
  2. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Place the pizza base on a baking tray.
    2. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce over the base.
    3. Add the BBQ chicken mixture evenly.
    4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
    5. Add onion slices and sweet corn.
  3. Bake the Pizza
    1. Place the pizza in the preheated oven.
    2. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  4. Serve
    1. Remove the pizza from the oven.
    2. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro
    3. Slice and serve hot.

Ingredients
  • 200g noodles
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Wash and slice the vegetables.
    2. Mince the garlic.
  2. Cook the Noodles
    1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions.
    2. Drain and set aside.
  3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables
    1. Heat oil in a pan.
    2. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    3. Add carrot, bell pepper, and cabbage.
    4. Cook until slightly tender.
  4. Combine and Season
    1. Add the cooked noodles to the pan.
    2. Pour in soy sauce.
    3. Season with salt and black pepper.
    4. Toss everything together and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Serve
    1. Transfer to a plate.
    2. Serve hot and enjoy!

Preparation Time:20 minutes
Difficulty Level:Easy
Servings:2 people 🍽️

Drinks
Mango Milkshake Hot Chocolote Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Peel and chop the mango into small pieces.
    2. Gather the milk, ice cream, and sugar.
  2. Blend the Milkshake
    1. Add the mango pieces to a blender.
    2. Pour in the chilled milk.
    3. Add vanilla ice cream and sugar.
    4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve
    1. Pour the milkshake into a glass.
    2. Add ice cubes if desired.
    3. Garnish with mango slices and serve immediately.
Preparation Time:10 minutes
Difficulty Level:Easy
Servings:2 glasses 🍹

Ingredients
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 50g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional)
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Gather the milk, cocoa powder, sugar, and dark chocolate.
    2. Chop the chocolate into small pieces.
  2. Make the Hot Chocolate
    1. Pour the milk into a saucepan.
    2. Heat over medium heat, stirring continuously.
    3. Add the chopped dark chocolate and stir until melted.
  3. Finish the Drink
    1. Add vanilla extract.
    2. Continue heating for 1–2 minutes without boiling.
  4. Serve
    1. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs.
    2. Top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.
    3. Serve warm and enjoy!
Preparation Time:10 minutes
Difficulty Level:Easy
Servings:2 cups ☕

Ingredients
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Wash and hull the strawberries.

Dessert
Chocolote Brownies Apple Pie Mango Mouse

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup (115 g) butter
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chocolate chips or chopped nuts
Preparation Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease or line an 8-inch (20 cm) square baking pan.
  3. Melt the butter and stir in the sugar.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Mix in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips or nuts if using.
  7. Spread batter into the pan.
  8. Bake for 20–25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  9. Cool completely before cutting.
Tips for extra fudgy brownies
  • Slightly underbake them.
  • Use dark cocoa powder for a richer flavor.
  • Add 1/2 cup melted dark chocolate to the batter.
Makes about16 brownies. 🍫

Ingredients For the Filling
  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
For the Crust
  • 2 pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
Preparation Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice.
  3. Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Add the apple filling evenly.
  5. Cover with the second crust and seal the edges. Cut a few slits on top.
  6. Brush the top with beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 45–50 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  8. Let the pie cool for at least 30 minutes before serving.
Serves:8
Preparation Time:20 minutes
Baking Time:45–50 minutes

Ingredients
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Preparation Steps
  1. Blend the mango pieces into a smooth puree.
  2. In a bowl, whip the heavy cream with sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the mango puree and lemon juice into the whipped cream.
  4. Spoon the mixture into serving glasses.
  5. Refrigerate for 2–3 hours until set.
  6. Garnish with fresh mango pieces before serving.
Serves:4
Preparation Time:15 minutes
Chilling Time:2–3 hours
