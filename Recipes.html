Main Dishes
Garlic herb Grilled chicken Creamy Chicken Pasta BBQ Chicken Pizza Vegetable Noodless

Ingredients
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges (optional)
Preparation Steps
  1. Prepare the Chicken
    1. Wash and pat dry the chicken breasts
    2. Season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Make the Herb Marinade
    1. Mix garlic, olive oil, oregano, thyme, and paprika in a bowl.
    2. Coat the chicken evenly with the mixture.
    3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill the Chicken
    1. Preheat the grill or grill pan.
    2. Cook the chicken for 6–8 minutes on each side.
    3. Ensure the chicken is fully cooked
  4. Serve
    1. Remove from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes.
    2. Garnish with lemon wedges
    3. Serve hot with vegetables, salad, or rice.
Cooking Time: 45 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy
Servings: 2 people 🍽️

Ingredients
  • 200g pasta
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Preparation Steps
  1. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Add the pasta and cook until al dente
    3. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Chicken
    1. Season the chicken with salt and black pepper.
    2. Heat olive oil in a pan
    3. Cook the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked.
  3. Make the Creamy Sauce
    1. Add minced garlic to the pan and sauté for 1 minute.
    2. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well.
    3. Add Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.
    2. Mix well until the pasta is evenly coated.
    3. Garnish with fresh parsley.
    4. Serve hot and enjoy!
Preparation Time:30 minutes
Difficulty Level:Easy
Servings:4 people 🍽️
